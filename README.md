Konnekt App
Konnekt is a personalized video conferencing platform that bridges the gap between global virtual meeting tools and the specific needs of organizations and teams by integrating personalization, cultural inclusivity, and automation.

Why It Matters
Many global video conferencing tools are generic and lack local relevance. Konnekt fills this gap by:  
Offering a culturally inclusive experience   
Helping teams collaborate more effectively  
Improving engagement and productivity: By personalizing the meeting experience and integrating collaborative tools, Konnekt ensures that participants are more focused, connected, and able to contribute meaningfully

Target Audience
Organizations and teams  
Small businesses and startups 
NGOs and non-profit organizations

Core Features
User Account & Dashboard
Secure user authentication via Google or manual email/password.  
Dashboard provides quick actions: Create Meeting, Join Meeting, or Schedule Meeting.

 Scheduling & Calendar Sync
Schedule meetings with automatic calendar sync (Google Calendar).  
Automatic reminders are sent before each meeting.

Meeting Management
Hosts can add co-hosts with limited controls.  
Manage participants: admit/remove, mute all, stop video sharing.  
Password-protect meetings and recordings.

File Sharing & Privacy Controls
Share documents and media from Google Drive, gallery, or the Files app.  
Option to share files with everyone or selected participants.

Localization & Accessibility
Live subtitles and translations in English, Yoruba, Igbo, Hausa, and Pidgin.  

Post-Meeting Analytics
Generate attendance reports with participant presence and duration.  

Monetization
Premium users access larger meeting capacity, additional recording space, and advanced analytics.  
Secure payments handled via Paystack.

Key Technologies
Frontend: React.js (Web), React Native (Mobile)
Backend: Node.js, Express.js 
Database:MongoDB
Real-time Communication: WebRTC and Socket.io 
Authentication & Security: JWT, OAuth, and role-based access control
Hosting & Deployment: AWS or Heroku 
Dev Tools & CI/CD: GitHub
